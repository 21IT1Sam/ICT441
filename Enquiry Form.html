Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where is the school located?

    • Australia School of Business is in Level 6, 6 Railway Pde, Burwood NSW 6203 Australia.

  • What courses does ASB offer?

    • ASB offers 5 courses, mainly focused on business management education.

  • What kind of school is ASB?

    • ASB is a vocational college that provides training and education above the minimum quality. The advantage of ASB over other universities is that they consider students' actual work as a part of the curriculum, which allows student teams to prepare for job hunting, interviews, and other situations.

  • How much does this school cost?

    • The tuition fee and all other fees differ based on the course. ASB gives various payment options and plans. For more information, please connect with us through chat and we will send the amount of the fee for your specific course of choice.

  • How long do courses take?

    • Course duration varies. You can visit the website to view course information, including duration

  • Is SSBT accepting international students?

    • Yes. ASB has always welcomed students from all over the world to participate in learning.
