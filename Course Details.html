Course Details

School Values

  • Students are always responsible for their actions, and when they fail to meet their standards, they will acknowledge and strive to become the best of themselves.
  • Students work with all their classmates and hope that everyone can learn and achieve success, which requires them to work together, compromise, and share in order to achieve success.
  • Students dare to face challenges bravely and help each other in difficult times of study and life.

    • Lesson Plan

  • Objective - Informed students of the content to be learned in the course and explained how to evaluate their learning content.
  • Materials - Textbooks, Notebook computer, Calculator, Worksheets, Pens, Notebooks, etc.
  • Background knowledge - Use multimedia to impart the background knowledge of the course to students to help them further understand the course content.
  • Direct instruction - Guide the class to learn the courses they will be studying. This will include impart course knowledge to students, have students read textbooks and take notes on the materials.
  • Guided teaching - Ask students to take notes on the worksheet and ask the teacher questions, continue learning in small groups, and finally complete the homework to gradually learn the course content.
  • Closure and assessment - Students can analyze and summarize the content they learned in the course of the day, evaluate their understanding of the content learned, and inform the teacher whether they have achieved the course objectives.
